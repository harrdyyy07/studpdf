Frequently Asked Questions

Are the study materials free? +
Yes, absolutely! Our mission is to make quality educational content accessible to all students. All our notes, question papers, and calculators are 100% free to use and download.
Are the notes updated to the latest VTU scheme? +
We try our best to keep materials updated for the 2021 and 2022 CBCS schemes. The scheme details will be mentioned appropriately on the subject pages. Always cross-verify topics with your official syllabus copy.
How accurate is the SGPA/CGPA calculator? +
Our calculator is strictly built according to the official VTU grade point logic (Absolute Grading System). If you enter the correct credits and marks, the result is highly accurate.
Can I contribute my own notes? +
Yes, we highly encourage student contributions! You can use the "Upload Notes" page linked in the footer to submit your scanned handwritten notes or typed PDFs. Our team will review and publish them.
I found a broken link or incorrect document. What should I do? +
If you encounter any issues, please reach out to us via the Contact Us page or join our WhatsApp/Telegram community groups to report the issue directly. We will fix it as soon as possible.
